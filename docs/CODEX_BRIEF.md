# Codex Brief — CareFi MEDUSDi Market Lab

## Objective

Build a polished investor-roadshow Streamlit app that shows how MEDUSDi can become both:

1. an investable medical-inflation exposure; and
2. a fee-generating AMM market operated by CareFi through liquidity provision.

## Brand and tone

- Brand: CareFi / MEDUSDi only. Do not fold this into Oriel.
- Tone: institutional fintech, concise, investor-facing.
- Avoid: claims-realization model, payer/provider settlement complexity, overly technical DeFi jargon.

> MEDUSDi is the asset. The AMM is the market. CareFi is the operator.

The demo should make investors understand why investing in CareFi is different from simply buying MEDUSDi on Uniswap: CareFi can own/operate the market layer, manage liquidity, earn LP fees, and build the spot/FV basis market around medical inflation.
